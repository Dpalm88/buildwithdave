Full Launch Package — How It Works

Your .com, start to finish. Here's exactly what happens.

No surprises. No technical headaches. I handle every step — and everything ends up in your name, not mine.

1
Day 1

We pick your domain name together

During our kickoff chat we'll brainstorm options — usually something like portlandplumbing.com or yourbizname.com. I'll check availability live and we'll lock one in.

💡 .com domains run ~$10–15/yr. That annual renewal stays with you.
2
Day 1

Domain is registered in your name

I purchase the domain through Namecheap using your email address — so it belongs to you from day one. You get the login credentials immediately. No lock-in, no dependency on me.

🔒 Your domain, your account. I never hold it on your behalf.
3
Day 1–2

Site is built & deployed to hosting

While DNS propagates (the internet "learns" your new address — usually a few hours, up to 24), I finish building your site and deploy it to Netlify's free hosting tier.

⚡ Netlify hosting is free forever for sites like yours. No monthly fee.
4
Day 2

Domain is pointed to your site

I connect your .com to your hosting — updating DNS nameservers so that typing yourbusiness.com takes visitors to your new site. Takes about 15 minutes of work on my end.

🌐 Once live, your site is reachable at your real address worldwide.
5
Day 2

SSL certificate activates automatically

Netlify provisions a free SSL certificate the moment DNS resolves — so your site loads as https:// (the padlock icon). Google ranks secure sites higher. Zero extra cost.

🔐 Free SSL via Let's Encrypt. Renews automatically every 90 days.
6
Day 2–3

15-min walkthrough & full handover

We hop on a quick screen share. I show you your Namecheap domain dashboard and your Netlify hosting panel. You have complete, independent access to everything. We're done — it's yours.

✅ After this call you need zero technical knowledge to keep your site running.
What if I already have a domain?
No problem — I can point your existing domain to the new site. The process is the same, just skipping the purchase step. No extra charge.
What does it cost me after launch?
Just your domain renewal — roughly $15/yr. Netlify hosting is free. That's it unless you add the optional Care Plan for updates.
Can I move the site to a different host later?
Yes, completely. You own the code and the domain. You can take it to any host you like, anytime. No penalties, no friction.
